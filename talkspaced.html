About Us

TalkspaceD is a spirited, powerful driving force of motivation to help you through the rollercoaster ride that we call life. We firmly believe that all human beings have great capacities that, when properly motivated and supported, can be mobilized to create a lasting impact on our society. We provide our audiences with what they actually need - A blended mixture of motivation, inspiration and entertainment. This is intended to equip them with the necessary tools to be better prepared to handle the hurdles life throws at them. If the hurdles are like boulders in the river of life, we provide the push to develop the willingness to not succumb to those ‘boulders’ and keep flowing with the river of life. We also propagate motivation by way of supporting and promoting new talents in any way we can.

How Will It Operate?

We work with a simplistic approach. We try to bring to you the views of informed men, the knowledge of learned men and the sagacity of wise men, and the perspective of a common man. This comes to you in the form of interviews, Facebook live sessions and videos about how the common man feels. We have designed events that are fully equipped to motivate you in a very relaxing way. This is the aim of TalkspaceD, a society that is motivated and full of stimulated, active, energetic, enthusiastic, happy people with a sense of purpose while being void of feelings like feeling uninterested, listless, dissatisfied, unresponsive, or even depressed.
